What is this?

Zeitspiegel (“time mirror”) is a mirror that runs a few seconds late: the screen shows the camera image from moments ago, horizontally flipped like a real mirror.

Why it helps dancers (and anyone training movement): you can’t watch yourself in a mirror while you move — looking at your reflection mid-turn breaks your posture, your spotting and your focus. With a delayed mirror you dance the phrase first, then look up and watch yourself do it, like a coach instantly replaying every attempt. No fumbling with a phone, no stopping the music.

How to use it: set the delay a little longer than the movement you’re working on, do the movement, then watch the screen. Liked an attempt? Save the last seconds as a video below.

Everything stays in this room: the video lives only in the device’s memory and is gone when you unplug it — nothing is recorded unless you download a clip.
